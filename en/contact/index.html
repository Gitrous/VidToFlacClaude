Before you write

If your video throws an error, include the file format and the conversion log message.

Include your browser and operating system so we can reproduce the problem.

Your files are never uploaded to any server: all processing happens 100% in your browser.

All processing happens on your device using ffmpeg.wasm — no data ever leaves your browser.
