VidToFLAC

Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

VidToFLAC (vidtoflac.tech) is a free web tool that converts the audio of video and audio files to the lossless FLAC format, directly in your browser. The service is operated independently and hosted on GitHub Pages.

2. Data we do NOT collect

The conversion tool runs 100% locally, inside your own browser, using FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly. This means:

  • Your video and audio files are never uploaded to any server.
  • We do not store, analyze or process the contents of your files.
  • We do not link the contents of your files to your identity or use them to build advertising profiles.
  • There is no user registration, no accounts, no passwords.

The site does display Google AdSense advertising and stores a few preferences in your browser's local storage; both are detailed in sections 3 and 4.

3. Data that is transmitted to third parties

Although the tool is entirely local, the simple act of visiting the website involves certain technical transmissions:

  • GitHub Pages (Microsoft): the site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may keep standard access logs (IP address, date/time, pages requested) in accordance with its own privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts: the site's typefaces are loaded from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com). Your IP address may be logged on Google's servers in accordance with its privacy policy.
  • unpkg (npm CDN): the FFmpeg.wasm engine is downloaded from unpkg.com on your first visit. Your IP address may be logged on unpkg's servers.
  • Google AdSense: the site is funded by ads served through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and identifiers to show ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites, as well as to measure their performance and prevent fraud. Learn more about how Google uses cookies in advertising. You can manage or turn off ad personalization from your Google ad settings or at aboutads.info/choices.

We have no control over the data logged by these third-party infrastructure and advertising providers.

4. Cookies and local storage

VidToFLAC does not use its own tracking or analytics cookies. However:

  • Third-party cookies (advertising): Google AdSense and its partners may set cookies or other identifiers in your browser to serve and measure ads, as described in section 3. In the European Economic Area, these personalized advertising cookies are only used after obtaining your consent through Google's cookie consent notice.
  • Local storage (localStorage): we save a few preferences and usage counters in your own browser (for example, the number of conversions performed and the free daily quota). This data never leaves your device, is not sent to us, and you can delete it by clearing the site's browsing data.

Refer to the policies of the third parties mentioned in section 3 for more information about their cookies.

5. Analytics services

We currently do not use any web analytics tool (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.). No data about user behavior within the site is recorded.

6. Your rights

Because we do not store any personally identifiable data, there is no profile of yours to access, rectify or delete. If you have any questions about how the third parties mentioned above process your data, you should contact them directly.

For any questions regarding this policy, you can write to us at hola@vidtoflac.tech.

7. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy when necessary. The date of the last change will always be shown at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after any change implies acceptance of the new version.

All processing happens on your device using ffmpeg.wasm — no data ever leaves your browser.
